About Me

My name is Bogdan Foca. I am a 20 year old computer science student at the Polytechnic University of Bucharest and a passionate programmer and game developer in my free time.

I had my first taste of programming when I was in 7th grade when a friend came to me and proposed we learned how to make our own games. We both liked games a lot since we were little so we began learning. We started by making small games in C.

9th grade marked the beggining of me actually programming. I started learning Unity. From then on I only got better and I found out that I like this so much that I could make games for the rest of my life. Since then I have been on several events and began many small projects of my own.

I have extensive experience in programming a video game, both singleplayer and multiplayer. I have knowledge in other software development and lower level stuff. I also have some experience with 2D art and 3D modeling in Blender.

  • Game Engines: Unity in C#, Unreal Engine in C++
  • OOP: C#, Java
  • Algorithm analisys and design in C and C++
  • Intel x86 assembly language
  • Low level networking in C
  • Functional languages: Racket, Haskel

I adore everything related to programming and learning. I view programming as very complex puzzle solving and in some way as an art and there is nothing I would love to do more.

Currently I am trying to get more serious about making games, learning increasingly complex stuff and releasing my games to the market. My dream would be to have my own indie studio.
