Jocul cu Urși

Global Game Jam Next 2019

Jocul cu Urși ("The Game with Bears") is a comically bad game where you play as an unamed superhero.

You must use the only super power you have, lifting things up at the cost of making a lot of noise to reach the end of the level.

You can download and play the game here: Jocul cu Urși

Development

This project was part of the event "Global Game Jam Next 2019". It was a smaller scale jam. It was 5 days long but we only worked during the day, from 10 to 16. I was one of the 2 programmers and I also worked on the sprites.

We were 4 people in the team, but we found ourselves with no artist. Because of that we decided to make a game that is comically bad.

We tailored the experience to be as badly made as posible, but on purpose, from the assets to the sound design, and even how the code works.

At the end of the event we held a presentation of our game and I can say that our objective was fullfilled. The audience was in tears from laughing when we were playing the game and we got praise saying that we nailed making a game that is bad on purpose.
