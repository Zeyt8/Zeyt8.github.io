NEET Rush

Global Game Jam 2019

You find yourself in the shoes of a social outcast, a man who sits all his day in front of the computer. All of a sudden it breaks.

You have to bring the PC to the repaird shop while avoiding people. If you stay close to them too much your anxiety will increase and you will return home. Fortunatelly there are bushes that you can hide into.

Try to bring your PC to repair to save your HOME.

You can download and play the game here: NEET Rush

Development

This project was part of the event "Global Game Jam 2019". The theme was "HOME". Our idea was that our character that stays in front of his computer all day, basically considers that his home.

It was the first time I participated in a game jam. To make things easy we decided to go for a 2D sidescroller. My first game jam was an eye opening experience for me and I gained a taste on what programming a game feels like.

In the night before the last day of the event we relised that even though the code was written we did no t have the art ready. Fortunately for us, there was another team full of artists that were missing a programmer, because he left the event.

We made a deal. 2 of them made art for us, while I coded their game. In that night I rewrote their entire game into Unity, as I was unfamiliar with their home made engine.

In the end both projects were finished on time.
