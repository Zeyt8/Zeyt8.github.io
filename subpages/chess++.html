Chess++

The game is a variation of 3 man chess. Unlike the normal variant, you have more pieces and attacks don't instantly destroy enemy pieces.

Your army is the classic 16 pieces, 2 row configuration. You can choose any of the available pieces to add as long as your total score is low enough.

Units don't instantly take enemy pieces. Pieces have health and they dissapear when their health reaches 0. Most new pieces have abilities that interact with the new mechanics.

The first player that manages to destroy any king wins the game.

You can download and play the game here: Chess++

Development

This project was made as my first Unreal Engine 4 project. I worked solo on it to learn the engine and deepen my c++ coding skills. It is mainly coded in c++. It took about 2 weeks to make.

From this project I have accustomed myself with the Unreal Engine 4 workflow. I gained knowledge in how to make a saving system, parse player input, work with multiple coordinates systems.

The game is not 100% finished, but it is playable with the default pieces.
