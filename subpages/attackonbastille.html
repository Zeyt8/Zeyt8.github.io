Attack on Bastille

The revolutionaries are mad at the king and they are coming to take down The Bastille.

You can hire royal troops or mercenaries to stomp on the revolt. Royal troops will drain your manpower, while mercenaries only cost you coins.

You have an arsenal of 3 types of units. Marksmen which stay on the roofs, swordmen which fight on the streets and barricades.

Last as long as you can. The revolution cannot be stopped, only delayed.

You can download and play the game here: Attack on Bastille

Development

I made this project together with a friend for our french class. It took us 2 days to see it completed. I was in charge of the programming and design and he did the models and animations.

It was a lot of fun researching the streets of Paris to find a good map design. The programming part went smoothly and I am proud of how good the game feels after only investing 2 days into it.

From this game I found that a simple, but focused design can easily make a fun game. From time to time I come back to play it and I enjoy it every time.
