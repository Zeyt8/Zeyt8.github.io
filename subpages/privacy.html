Jam de casă 2020

You play as a 36 year old hacker. Your task is to infiltrate your target's computer and collect information based on the task on hand. Tasks range from copying files to your USB stick to learning information about your target about which you will be intoregated.

You navigate on a computer, through its folders, looking through the files. Your time is limited though, so you have to be fast.

You can download and play the game here: Privacy

Development

This project was part of the event "Jam de casă". We had 1 week to make a game based on the theme "You only have 5 minutes". The staff of the event played these games on a stream and all donations were donated to COVID19 help.

We made a computer based on Manjaro that you can navigate through to find files and clues. I was in charge of most of the programming. In that week, I recreated the linux interface, directory system, photo viewer and audio player inside the game. I also took care of the level creation and asset integration.
