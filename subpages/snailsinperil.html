Snails in Peril

Week Sauce (Apr 2022)

You have to go through a series of levels while controlling 2 snails with different abilities.

The snails work together to evade the evil snails who want them captured and to unlock the path forward.

Stealth and subtilty is the key element here as evil snails are way too powerfull to be taken up front. Puzzles will be challenging the cooperation between the snails.

You can download and play the game here: Snails in Peril

Development

This project was part of the event "Week Sauce (Apr 2022)". The theme was the name of a band of our own choosing, ours being Snail. I was the programmer of the team.

We had any 7 days, not necessarily consecutive days in the month of april to make the game. Due to our busy schedule, we only used 3 of those days. One month after the jam we worked on the game for another 3 days. We redid the entire design and implemeted the remaining ideas we had.

Within this project I handled the general direction of the game. As the only programmer I took part in all of the development pipeline of the game. My work ranged from player controlls to AI behaviour using state machines to procedural meshes and animation.

I was in charge of the level design of the reworked level we made after the jam.
