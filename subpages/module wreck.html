Module Wreck

Global Game Jam 2020

You're on a mission in space. Your ship is a cheap wreck.

To reach the end of the level team up with your partner in fighting enemies while repairing the modules of your ship that keep malfunctioning. Good luck!

You can download and play the game here: Module Wreck

Development

This project was part of the event "Global Game Jam 2020". The theme was "Repair". I was one of the 2 programmers.

This was the second time I attended Global Game Jam. With the experience from last time everything worked smoother. It took us a while to fixate on the final design, but after that the game just fell into place.

The other participants loved our game and we got the second most votes for the best game.
